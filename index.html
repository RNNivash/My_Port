CHAT NEST APP

Chat Nest 🏡 - Your Neighborhood Communication Hub! 📱 made with Flutter and Dart ,It is a mobile application designed to facilitate communication among neighbors who are registered users of the app. Users can register with their email and password, utilizing Firebase for authentication. Upon successful login, they are redirected to the chat screen where they can engage in conversations with their neighbors who are also registered on the platform.

FINGER DASHER GAME APP

Finger Dasher 🎮 - A multiplayer game app developed with Flutter and Dart, where two players compete by tapping their respective color areas. Each tap earns 5 points, and the goal is to cover 80% of the screen with your color to win. The winner's points are displayed, and a restart button allows for endless rematches!

FACE BASED ATTENDANCE

Our system is an automated face-based attendance management solution. Users input their image and name to train the system for facial recognition.
